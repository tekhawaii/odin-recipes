Tony's Chocolate Milk

Description:

Chcolate milk- needs no further introduction. friend recipe btw.. shouldn't be spilling this (pun intendend) but I will for you guys.

Ingredients:

  • milk
  • chocolate
  • banana

Steps (EASY THREE STEP PROCESS (AGAIN!)!):

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt the chocolate and heat up milk in a microwave or something
  3. Combine and let sit

Now you have super tasty gourmet choclate milk!
